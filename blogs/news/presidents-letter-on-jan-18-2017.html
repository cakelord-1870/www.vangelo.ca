PRESIDENT'S LETTER ON JAN.18, 2017

Jan.18th, 2017

Dear Customer,

It gives me great pleasure to invite you to visit Vangelo Footwear at the coming Shoe Shows. Vangelo Footwear has been supplying excellent lines of Men’s, Women’s and Kids’ footwear to retailers for many years. Vangelo will exhibit our Fall/Winter 2017 collections as well as Vangelo’s Spring/Summer 2017 collections in stock at a series of the coming shows:

 

Montreal Boot Show: Room#223; Jan. 28-31, 2017

Quality Suites, 2035 Autoroute Des Laurentides. Laval, QC H7S 1Z6

London Shoe Show: Room#290; Jan. 29-30, 2017

Four Points Sheraton, London, ON

Doubletree International Centre, Toronto, ON

Toronto Gift Fair: HALL#12 North BLDG -Booth#12317; Jan. 29 - Feb. 02, 2017

Toronto Congress Centre, North BLDG, 650 Dixon Rd., Toronto, ON

Edmonton Fall 2017 Footwear Buying Market: Room# 2803; Feb.03-05, 2017

Double Tree Hilton, West Edmonton, AB

Ottawa Boot Show: Suite#200 and #217; Feb.05-06, 2017

Best Western Plus - Ottawa City Centre, Ottawa, ON

Moncton Boot Mart: Room# 112; Feb.11-13, 2017

Days Inn & Suites, Moncton, NB

Four Points Sheraton, London, ON

Midwest Buy Mart: Booth#E-12 Hall D; Feb.25-27, 2017

Travel Lodge, Sudbury, ON

 

You are our most valued customer. We look forward to meeting and serving you the best!
